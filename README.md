# 📊 FUTURE_DS_02
Customer Churn Analysis (Excel)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes customer churn data to identify patterns, retention drivers, and business insights.

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Excel (Pivot Tables, Charts, Dashboard)

## 📊 Key Analysis
- Churn by Contract
- Churn by Tenure
- Churn by Payment Method
- Churn by Monthly Charges
- Retention Analysis

## 📈 Key Insights
- Month-to-month customers churn more
- New customers are at higher risk
- High charges increase churn
- Electronic check users churn more

## 💡 Recommendations
- Promote long-term contracts
- Improve onboarding
- Encourage auto-payments
- Focus on high-risk customers

##💼 Business Questions & Answers

 Why are customers leaving the platform?
👉 Customers are mainly leaving due to:
Month-to-month contracts (no long-term commitment)
High monthly charges
Poor onboarding experience in early stages
Payment issues (electronic check users)

Which customer segments are most likely to churn?
👉 High-risk segments include:
New customers (0–1 year tenure)
Month-to-month contract users
High monthly charge customers
Electronic check payment users

❓ How long do customers typically stay active?
👉 Observations:
Many customers churn within the first year
Long-term customers (4+ years) show strong retention
Retention increases with tenure

❓ What actions can improve customer retention?
👉 Recommended actions:
Promote long-term contracts with discounts
Improve onboarding for new customers
Encourage auto-payment methods
Provide offers for high-value customers
Focus on early-stage engagement
